Who We Are

The AI Governance & Enablement team ensures the ethical, responsible, and secure use of AI across the organization by aligning with risk, legal, privacy, security, talent, and compliance standards. Grounded in the NIST AI RMF, we provide risk-based guidance, foundational processes, and structured oversight so AI solutions can scale safely and confidently. We partner with project teams from intake through trial, proof of concept, and production—developing policies, standards, and observability practices while ensuring risks are documented, reviewed, and addressed through structured assessments and the Architecture Review Board. In parallel, we equip employees with training, tools, and AI assistant enablement to build AI literacy and apply AI effectively in their roles.

Why Our Work Matters

AI is transforming how the company operates, innovates, and serves its stakeholders. Our team ensures that this transformation is both responsible and impactful by embedding strong governance and practical enablement into every stage of AI adoption. By building an AI-fluent workforce, we empower employees to use AI confidently and responsibly—unlocking productivity, improving outcomes, and reducing operational and compliance risk while accelerating value creation across all roles.
