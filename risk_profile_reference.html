AI Risk Level Definitions
🟢 Low Risk
Score 17 – 36

Standard AI solutions with minimal organizational risk — typically third-party tools, public data, limited autonomy, and restricted access. No additional governance engagement is typically required; standard operational controls apply.

🟡 Moderate Risk
Score 37 – 53

Elevated risk factors requiring additional oversight. The Architecture Review Board reviews these assessments, and additional documentation, controls, or monitoring may be needed before deployment.

🔴 High Risk
Score 54 – 85

Significant organizational risk. A formal Architecture Review Board review and leadership-level approval are mandatory before proceeding. Enhanced controls, ongoing monitoring, compliance documentation, and periodic re-assessments are required throughout the solution's lifecycle.
