Overview

This page provides a central view of Generative AI tools that have been reviewed by the AI Governance & Enablement Team. It helps employees understand which tools have been evaluated and the conditions under which they are currently approved for use across the company, including legacy Vivint.

Start Here Before Introducing a New AI Tool or Vendor

Please follow the steps below to ensure responsible, secure, and compliant AI use at the company:

  1. Review the AI Governance Hub for guidance on AI oversight, risk assessment, standards, and approved processes.
  2. Follow The company's standard supply chain and vendor onboarding process for procurement and contracting.
  3. Check the AI Inventory List (below) to determine whether the tool or vendor has already been reviewed or approved.
  4. Contact the AI Governance & Enablement Team at AIGovernance@parivedasolutions.com with questions about governance requirements or to request that a tool be added to the inventory once the vendor onboarding process has been initiated.
Note: The AI Governance & Enablement Team, in collaboration with CIC, evaluates tools based on their AI capabilities and associated risks, as well as alignment with Pariveda's enterprise strategy and employee needs. This list is not intended to be a comprehensive inventory of all vendor AI capabilities.
